Laboratory of Computational Quantum Many-body Theory

We are a research group at the Centre Européen de Sciences Quantiques (CESQ), an interdisciplinary European quantum science center in Strasbourg, France. We are part of the Institut de science et d'ingénierie supramoléculaires (ISIS), at the Université de Strasbourg. Welcome to our homepage, where you can learn about our research activities and team.
