Welcome!

To the homepage of the 54th edition of the EGAS conference. EGAS is an annual event with a long tradition, organized since 1969 by the European Group on Atomic Systems (https://www.eps-egas.org) of the European Physical Society. The 2023 event will take place in the heart of Europe from June 18-22 at the University of Strasbourg.

The Conference will bring together experts from across Europe and the World on the topics of EGAS:

  • Fundamental physics  
  • Cold gases and quantum fluids
  • Quantum optics, quantum information and quantum simulation
  • Frequency and time domain spectroscopy and metrology
  • Applications to astrophysics, plasma physics, biophysics, clusters . . 
  • Atom-like systems

A focus topic of the 54th edition will be on quantum technology applications of AMO Physics.

The Venue

Strasbourg, the capital of Alsace, is situated in the region Grand Est alongside the romantic Rhine river on the French-German border. It is a historic, international and academic city, and boasts an eclectic mix of old and new: its historic city center (a UNESCO World Heritage Site) overlooked by the Notre-Dame-de-Strasbourg cathedral is contrasted by the modern seat of the European Parliament.
